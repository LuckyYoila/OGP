Thematic Areas

Fiscal Transparency

To ensure open contracting in public procurement in order to optimise financial efficiency and minimise/prevent financial recklessness/leakages and also improve the participation of citizens in the budgeting process.
Commitments:
1. Improving Transparency in Procurement Processes.
2. Improving Participatory Budgeting.

Peace And Security

To create and maintain a peaceful environment for development by...
Commitment: Establishing an Independent Peace and Security Trust Fund.

Gender

To enhance participation of women in governance and decision-making processes.
Commitment: Improving Gender Equality through the establishment of a Gender and Equal Opportunities Commission.

Citizens Engagement

To create the space for citizens, citizen organisations and the media to thrive, express themselves and participate in the different stages of the policy making process without fear or intimidation.
Commitment: Establishing Citizens Dialogue Mechanisms for improved Accountability and Transparency.

Extractive Transparency

Increasing Transparency and Accountability in Extractive Sector through the establishment of the Plateau State Extractives Transparency Forum (PSETF).
Commitment: Increasing Transparency and Accountability in Extractive Sector.
